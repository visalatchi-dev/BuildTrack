Access control

About
Who We Are

We are a passionate team focused on improving construction site management through simple and effective digital solutions. Based in Tamil Nadu, we aim to help construction teams reduce miscommunication, improve productivity, and manage projects more efficiently. Our platform, BuildTrack, is designed with real-world site challenges in mind, making it easy for admins, supervisors, and workers to collaborate seamlessly.
